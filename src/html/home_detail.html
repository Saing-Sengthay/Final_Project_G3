About This Event

Join us for the most exciting basketball tournament of the year! Universities from across Cambodia will compete in this thrilling 3-day tournament. Whether you're a player or a spectator, this event promises non-stop action and incredible displays of athleticism.

The tournament features both men's and women's divisions, with prizes for the top teams. All skill levels are welcome, and we'll have professional referees ensuring fair play throughout the competition.

What's Included:

  • Official tournament jersey
  • Lunch and refreshments
  • Professional photography
  • Awards ceremony
  • Certificate of participation

Requirements:

  • Must be currently enrolled in a university
  • Valid student ID required
  • Medical clearance form
  • Sports insurance (provided if needed)
